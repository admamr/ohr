Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

The information provided on this page is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

The Ohr Foundation is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Foundation's website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:
Information You Provide Directly: When you contact us through website forms (such as the "Contact Us" form), sign up for a newsletter, or register for an event, you may provide us with personal details such as your full name, email address, and phone number.
Technical and Usage Data: Our systems may automatically collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and pages you visited on the website.
Cookies: The website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve the browsing experience, analyze traffic, and ensure information security.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- Responding to inquiries received through the website.
- Managing registration for Foundation events and activities.
- Improving and maintaining the website and user experience.
- Sending updates and relevant information (subject to your consent).
- Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your personal information is based on the following legal grounds: your consent (e.g., when submitting a form), the necessity to perform actions at your request, the legitimate interests of the Foundation (such as website security and service improvement), or a legal obligation applicable to us.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers and subcontractors who assist us in operating the website and activities (such as hosting providers, email services, and analytical tools), solely for the purpose of providing these services and subject to their commitment to confidentiality and data security.

5. Donations and Payments

If donations are processed through the website, they are handled by secure third-party clearing providers. Credit card details are not stored on the Foundation's servers. The use of clearing services is subject to the privacy policy and terms of use of those providers.

6. Data Security

We implement accepted technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, exposure, or destruction. However, no system is completely immune, and therefore we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law (e.g., for accounting and tax reporting).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request review of the personal information we hold about you, request its correction if inaccurate, or request its deletion (subject to legal limitations). To exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Children's Privacy

The website is not intended for children under a certain age without parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental or guardian consent. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with information without consent, please report it to us, and we will handle it accordingly.

10. External Links

This privacy policy applies to this website only. The site may contain links to other websites not under our control. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any website you visit.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always appear on this page, with the last updated date at the top.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, requests, or comments regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:
Via the Contact Us page on the website.
