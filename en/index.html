"Ohr" Association

Empowering Druze Youth Through Education

The Israeli Druze Association for the Commemoration of the Memory of the Late Brigadier General Munir Amar - works to promote peace and coexistence in Israel, education and culture in Druze society, and its representation before the international Jews community.

Our Vision

The "Ohr" association was founded in memory of the late Brigadier General Munir Amar, a leader and visionary who worked to promote peace, education, and culture among the Druze community and in Israeli society as a whole.

Education & Excellence

Providing scholarships to students and encouraging academic excellence among young members of the Druze community.

Community & Leadership

Developing young leadership and social involvement to strengthen community resilience.

Peace & Coexistence

Promoting values of peace, tolerance, and shared living among all sectors of Israeli society.

Who was Munir Amar

Brigadier General Munir Amar was an officer, a leader, and an educator who worked his entire life for the State and the Druze community in Israel. His legacy of commitment, service, and coexistence is the foundation of the Ohr Foundation's activities.
