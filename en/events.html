Events at Ohr Foundation

Events, Ceremonies, and Community Activities

Throughout the year, the Foundation holds a wide variety of educational events, community meetings, and official ceremonies, with aim of strengthening the bond between young Druze and Israeli society as a whole, and cultivating a future generation of leadership and at the monument of the late Colonel Munir Amar.
