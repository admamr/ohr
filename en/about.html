Our Story

About Ohr Foundation

Our activities focus on creating bridges – between tradition and progress, between the village and the academy, and between Druze society and Israeli society as a whole. We aspire to see our graduates integrating into key positions in the economy, academia, and Israeli public life, proud of their identity and working for the common good.

Vision and Path

The "Ohr" Foundation was founded in memory of Brigadier General Munir Amar, a leader and man of vision, who worked to promote peace, education, and culture among the Druze community and in Israeli society as a whole. The Foundation promotes Munir's values through granting unique scholarships to Druze students, educational and cultural initiatives, and strengthening ties between the Druze community and World Jewry. Our vision is an equal and cohesive society, where values of mutual respect and cooperation are at the center. By promoting education and culture, the "Ohr" Foundation aspires to encourage members of the Druze community to integrate and contribute to Israeli society, and to build bridges of understanding with World Jewry.

Our Areas of Activity

We operate on three main levels to achieve our goals

Education & Scholarships

Granting scholarships to outstanding students and those in need, and academic accompaniment throughout the path to the degree.

Young Leadership

Cultivating a young managerial and leadership reserve, ready to take responsibility and lead social processes.

Community & Society

Strengthening community resilience through volunteering, giving, and projects for both the elderly and young populations.

Our Inspiration

All the Foundation's activities are done in the light and spirit of Brigadier General Munir Amar. A man of people, an admired commander, and a leader who saw education and love of mankind as supreme values.

Munir's Story

The Ohr Foundation works to commemorate the memory of Brigadier General Munir Amar and to advance young men and women from Druze society through education, leadership, and community.
